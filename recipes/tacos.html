Ground Beef Tacos

Justin's Famous Tacos

These tex-mex style tacos will have you coming back for more. Cumin and chili powder dominate the seasoning of the ground beef, but the condiments complete these delicous tacos.

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 packet of taco seasoning
  • 1 roma tomato
  • 1 medium red onion
  • shredded Lettuce
  • shredded cheese
  • 1 lime
  • 10-12 corn tortillas

Steps

  1. Dice tomato and onion to preferred and set aside.
  2. Cook ground beef in skillet until fully browned.
  3. Add packet of seasoning and 2 tbs of water to cooked ground beef and stir until incorporated. Add more water if neccesary.
  4. Heat tortillas in microwave for 30 seconds or until warm.
  5. Assemble the tacos. Add a scoop of seasoned beef to your tortilla. Add cheese, lettuce, tomato, and onion.
  6. Cut lime in half and squeeze juice over assembled taco.
  7. Enjoy your delicous tacos.
