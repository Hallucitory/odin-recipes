Easy Nachos

Bacon Jalapeno Nachos

Nachos made in a microwave can't be this good. Quick, homemade nachos at your fingertips.

Ingredients

  • mexican blend shredded cheese
  • 1 jalapeno
  • pre-prepared bacon bits
  • corn tortilla chips

Steps

  1. Plug in and setup your microwave.
  2. Dice jalapeno into thin slices.
  3. Arrange tortilla chips in a thin layer on a microwave-safe plate.
  4. Cover tortilla chips lightly with shredded cheese. Add slice jalapenos and bacon bits on top.
  5. Microwave in 30 second intervals until cheese melts.
  6. Enjoy your nachos!
