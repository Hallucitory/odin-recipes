Buttery Baked Potato

Quick and easy baked potatos that will change your diet. A delicous, healthy recipe that keeps your calories low.

Ingredients

  • 1 russet potato
  • Kerrygold butter
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Preheat over to 450 degrees fahrenheit.
  2. Wash and scrub potato.
  3. Poke a few holes in the potato using a fork or knife.
  4. Place on baking sheet and bake at 450 degrees fahrenheit for 45 minutes or until softness of your liking.
  5. Cut the potato down the middle being careful of hot steam.
  6. Add 1 tbs of butter and mix with a fork.
  7. Salt to preferrence and enjoy your delicious baked potato.
